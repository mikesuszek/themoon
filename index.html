I aim to rip away the moon
I’ll tear it from the sky
I’ll snatch it from amongst the stars
And steal it from our eyes
 
How does one plan to finish this
Abominated plan?
A theft, what’s left? No hope or cause
To justify your scam.
 
My purpose is to free our minds,
To save our sorry souls.
To keep us all from staring at
Unreal and foolish goals.
 
To spend our days, our precious few,
Without a single dream,
It’s sacrilege, contempt, abuse,
And everything between.
 
Deny as much as you prefer--
I don’t know what you speak.
Facades just hold back greater truths.
The truth is what I seek.
 
What truths of yours are facts at all?
The facts, we’ll never know.
What good are heads stuck in the sand?
By dreaming, we might grow.
 
Without the moon, we never reach
Beyond our atmosphere.
It crushes, wipes, exonerates
Our limits and our fears.
